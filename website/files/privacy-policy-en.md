# Privacy policy

**Last updated:** April 9, 2026

This policy explains how we handle personal data related to **qooti** and **bloot**. The service is operated by an **independent individual** (self-employed operator). Questions: [Telegram](https://t.me/blootsupport).

## 1. Who collects data

We collect data to provide bloot/qooti services. Certain processing may involve technical providers (hosting, email, analytics) under appropriate agreements.

## 2. Categories of data

- **Account:** username, email, internal ID, language preference.
- **Technical:** IP address, device/browser signals, security logs (for security and reliability).
- **Billing/subscription:** payment date, amount, description (minimal records from the payment provider).
- **Content:** media you save may remain **on your device** locally; where content is uploaded to our systems, handling follows the applicable product terms.

## 3. Purposes and legal bases

We use data to operate accounts, secure services, provide support, and comply with legal obligations. Legal bases may include **contract** and **legitimate interests** (e.g., abuse prevention and service protection).

## 4. Retention

We retain data as long as necessary for the purposes above and as required by law. Logs and technical records are typically kept for shorter periods.

## 5. Third parties

We may use providers for email delivery, hosting, or analytics. They process data only as needed to deliver those services.

## 6. Your rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have rights to **access**, **rectify**, **erase**, or **restrict** processing. Contact us on Telegram to exercise these rights where available.

## 7. Children

Our services are not directed at children. If you believe we collected a child’s data, contact us.

## 8. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be communicated on the site.
