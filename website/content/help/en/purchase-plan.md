# Purchase a plan

1. **Sign in** to your bloot account.
2. Open **[Dashboard → Billing](/dashboard/billing)** and pick a qooti package.
3. Message **[@blootsupport](https://t.me/blootsupport) on Telegram** for payment instructions and activation.
4. Prices are listed in **UZS**; taxes and FX differences may apply.

Questions: [Telegram](https://t.me/blootsupport).
